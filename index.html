What is Trezor.io/start?

Trezor.io/start is the online gateway to initializing your Trezor hardware wallet. This setup page plays a pivotal role in the Trezor ecosystem, offering a streamlined way to get your device up and running. It provides instructions for connecting your Trezor, creating a PIN, and generating a recovery seed—key steps in securing your digital assets.

How to Access Trezor.io/start

To begin, navigate to Trezor.io/start on your browser. Ensure you’re using a secure and up-to-date web browser. The page will guide you through connecting your Trezor device to your computer. Follow the on-screen prompts to initiate the setup process and proceed through each step.

Step-by-Step Guide to Setting Up Trezor

  1. Connect Your Trezor: Plug your Trezor hardware wallet into your computer using the provided USB cable.
  2. Visit Trezor.io/start: Open the Trezor.io/start page and follow the instructions to install any necessary software.
  3. Create a PIN: Choose a secure PIN to protect your device.
  4. Backup Your Recovery Seed: Write down your recovery seed on the provided card and store it safely.

Troubleshooting Common Issues

If you encounter issues during setup, ensure your Trezor is correctly connected and your browser is up-to-date. Common issues include connection problems or difficulties with the PIN setup. Refer to the Trezor support page for troubleshooting tips.

Security Tips for Using Trezor

To maintain security, never share your recovery seed with anyone. Choose a strong, unique PIN for your Trezor and store your recovery seed in a safe, secure location. Regularly update your device's firmware to protect against vulnerabilities.
